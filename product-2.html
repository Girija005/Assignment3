HOW DO WE MAKE THEM:

Each PALI basket is individually handwoven in a remote village in Myanmar, from new pallet strapping made from recycled plastics. Plastic straps are collected by locals from streets and beaches, recycled into pallets, then made into rolls for weaving.

The traditional Burmese weaving techniques have been passed down through generations. Every basket sold enables our ongoing support of the artisans, village families and local Burmese communities.

All baskets are fully recyclable at the end of their very long lives, please contact us for more information.
